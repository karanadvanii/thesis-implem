Morning news!!

Today we are announcing at a press roundtable (for regional media / 9am at Bohnenmeister) that the city of Chemnitz is the first major city in Germany to successfully introduce an employee app.
Our press release says:
"This initiative to digitize the city’s internal communications was born out of a desire to provide all employees with up-to-date and relevant information, no matter where they were. As the latest numbers prove, this interest flows both ways – around 1,500 users (roughly one third of the city’s dispersed employees) have already registered with the app since it was launched at the end of August 2019. From kindergarten teachers to office workers and firefighters, they can all be reached within minutes."
